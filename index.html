Terry Fox Blog

by: Isaac, Derek and Anthony

Terrance Stanley "Terry" Fox was born July 28, 1958. In 1980 he ran with one leg having been amputated, he started his run across canada (Marathon Of Hope) to raise money and awareness for cancer research. His cancer eventually forced him to end his run at 143 days and 5,373 Km and unfortunately, he died. His efforts resulted in a lasting, worldwide legacy.
